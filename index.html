Faclair na Gàidhlig nphr.

  1. the Dictionary of the Scottish Gaelic Language
  2. an inter-university initiative to create a comprehensive dictionary of Scottish Gaelic on historical principles by 2045
  • nominative Clàraidh Faclair na Gàidhlig cunntas iomlan air eachdraidh na Gàidhlig agus cultar nan Gàidheal, bho na teacsaichean as tràithe a th’ againn ann an Gàidhlig gus an latha an-diugh, a’ cur Gàidhlig na h-Alba ann an co-theacsa le Gàidhlig na h-Èireann agus le Albais. Faclair na Gàidhlig will document fully the history of the Gaelic language and culture from the earliest manuscript material onwards, placing Gaelic in context with Irish and Scots.
  • dative Is e iomairt eadar-oilthighean a th’ ann am Faclair na Gàidhlig, anns a bheil Oilthigh Obar Dheathain, Oilthigh Dhùn Èideann, Oilthigh Ghlaschu, Oilthigh Shrath Chluaidh agus Sabhal Mòr Ostaig OGE an sàs. Faclair na Gàidhlig is an inter-university initiative by the Universities of Aberdeen, Edinburgh, Glasgow, Strathclyde and Sabhal Mòr Ostaig UHI.
  • genitive Thèid Ionad Faclair na Gàidhlig a chur air bhog ann an 2022, le làrach-lìn ùr agus na ciad eisimplearan dhen obair fhaclaireachd. The Centre for Faclair na Gàidhlig is being launched in 2022, with a new website and the first samples of the lexicographic work.

[qv. faclair n.m. ‘dictionary’, Gàidhlig n.f. ‘the Gaelic language’]
